About the shop

est. 2026

Wang Enterprises is a one-person 3D printing operation building custom, made-to-order pieces for collectors, gamers, and anyone who wants something that doesn't exist off the shelf yet.

Every order is printed on a Bambu Lab A1 Mini, tuned and tested piece by piece. That means smaller batches, tighter tolerances, and a real person checking each print before it ships.

Based between Waterloo and Ottawa-Gatineau, shipping across Canada and the US through Chit Chats.
